# 07. Call Logs
Status: completed
## 1) What this module is
Call Logs is where each employee sees their own call history in CRM.  
Calls are synced from the mobile app installed on that employee’s phone (`call-log-mobile-app`).

Example: Employee A sees Employee A’s calls only. (admin can see everyone's call logs)

## 2) What staff can do here
- Open their personal list of synced calls
- Search and filter calls to find a specific conversation
- Click a call to review details before taking action
- Create a **Ticket** from a call when it is a support issue
- Create a **Lead** from a call when it is a sales opportunity

## 3) How to use (step-by-step)
1. Open **Call Logs** from the CRM menu.
![Screenshot](/docs/screenshots/07-call-logs/01-call-logs-list-view.png)
2. Find the call you want (date, number, or status filters can help).
![Screenshot](/docs/screenshots/07-call-logs/02-call-logs-filters.png)
3. Click the call to open its details.
![Screenshot](/docs/screenshots/07-call-logs/03-call-detail-view.png)
4. Choose one action:
   - **Create Ticket** for service/support follow-up
![Screenshot](/docs/screenshots/07-call-logs/04-create-ticket-from-call.png)
   - **Create Lead** for new business follow-up
![Screenshot](/docs/screenshots/07-call-logs/05-create-lead-from-call.png)
5. CRM takes you to the selected module and opens the creation form.
6. The form already includes call context, so staff can finish and save quickly.

## 4) Important buttons/options
- **Call Logs**: Opens your own synced call list
- **Create Ticket**: Opens a new ticket form with call details carried over
- **Create Lead**: Opens a new lead form with call details carried over
- **Search and Filters**: Helps you find the right call faster
- **Call Detail View**: Where you review the call before choosing next action

## 5) Daily best practices
- Confirm your mobile app is active so calls sync regularly
- Check your Call Logs at least once daily
- Open each important call and create the correct follow-up record
- Use **Ticket** for support needs and **Lead** for sales opportunities
- Complete and save forms right after the call while details are fresh

## 6) Common mistakes to avoid
- Assuming all staff can see all calls (call logs are employee-specific)
- Using the wrong follow-up path (Ticket vs Lead)
- Skipping call review and creating records from memory
- Leaving important calls without creating a Ticket or Lead
- Delaying updates until details are forgotten
