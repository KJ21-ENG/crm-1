# Email Account
Status: pending

## 1) What this module is
Email Account is where office email connections are managed for sending and receiving CRM communication. It supports shared business communication from official channels.

## 2) What staff can do here
- Add or update office email account details.
- Select which account is used for outgoing messages.
- Keep communication channels current and reliable.

## 4) Important buttons/options
- **New**: Adds a new office email account.
- **Save**: Stores email account settings.
- **Set as default**: Uses this account as the main sender.
- **Test / Send test**: Checks if outgoing email works.

## 5) Daily best practices
- Keep only active, approved office accounts enabled.
- Review default sender settings after team changes.
- Use clear account naming so staff choose the correct sender.

## 6) Common mistakes to avoid
- Using personal email accounts for office communication.
- Setting the wrong default sender.
- Leaving old accounts active when no longer needed.
- Skipping the test message after updates.
