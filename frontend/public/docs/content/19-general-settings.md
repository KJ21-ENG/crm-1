# 19. General Settings
Status: completed
## 1) What this module is
General Settings is the central place to configure basic, system-wide information for your CRM instance. These configurations control how the CRM identifies itself visually to your branch and what base settings are used by default across various actions.

## 2) What staff can do here
- Update official company details used across the system, like the CRM **Brand name**.
- Set the **Default Referral Code** which gets automatically filled in new leads.
- **Change the Logo:** Update the image that appears in the top left of the CRM sidebar.
- **Change the Favicon:** Update the small image that appears in the browser tab next to the page title.
- Configure global "Home actions" that appear across the user interface.

## 3) How to use (step-by-step)
1. Open the user dropdown menu in the top-left sidebar (under 'CRM').
2. Select **Settings** to open the Settings modal.
3. Click on the **General** tab from the left sidebar of the Settings panel.
![Screenshot](/docs/screenshots/19-general-settings/01-general-settings.png)
4. **Changing Brand Details:**
   - Modify the "Brand name" or "Default Referral Code" text fields. 
5. **Changing the Logo & Favicon:**
   - Under **Logo**, click **Change** to upload a new image from your computer (Recommended: 32x32 px in PNG or SVG format).
   - Under **Favicon**, click **Change** to upload a new browser icon (Recommended: 32x32 px in PNG or ICO format).
   - You can also click **Remove** to clear the current image.
6. Once changes are made, notice that the **Update** button at the top right becomes active, and a "Not Saved" notification might appear next to the title.
7. Click the **Update** button in the top right corner to apply and save your changes system-wide.
8. (Optional) You may need to refresh your browser (Cmd/Ctrl + R) to verify that the Favicon and Logo have fully updated.

## 4) Important buttons/options
- **Change (Upload):** Opens a file selector to upload a new logo or favicon image.
- **Remove:** Deletes the specific uploaded image.
- **Update:** Saves any modifications across all sections in the General Settings.
- **Default Referral Code:** Enter a code that will be automatically populated for all new leads created in the system.
- **Home actions Grid:** Allows configuring the shortcut menus available in the CRM's global dropdowns.

## 5) Daily best practices
- Use clear, correct dimensions for Logo and Favicon so the CRM appears professional to all internal teams.
- Keep the Brand Name consistent with what team members expect to see.
- After saving structural changes like the Default Referral Code, confirm with team leaders to ensure it’s expected.

## 6) Common mistakes to avoid
- Uploading a very large image for the Favicon or Logo, which might render poorly or slow down initial loads.
- Forgetting to click the **Update** button (top right) after uploading an image or typing a change.
- Changing the "Home actions" routes without confirming they actually represent valid URLs in the system.
