# General Settings
Status: completed
## 1) What this module is
General Settings is the place where your organization sets basic system-wide information. These choices affect how the office uses the CRM every day.

## 2) What staff can do here
- Update official company details used across the system.
- Set office preferences such as working hours and default behavior.
- Keep shared settings consistent for all teams.

## 3) How to use (step-by-step)
1. Open **General Settings** from the settings area.

5. Select **Save**.

## 4) Important buttons/options
- **Edit**: Enables changes.
- **Save**: Applies new settings.
- **Cancel**: Leaves settings unchanged.
- **Default options**: Controls what appears automatically for staff.

## 5) Daily best practices
- Check settings after policy changes or office updates.
- Keep naming, formatting, and contact details standardized.
- Record who changed what and why in internal notes.

## 6) Common mistakes to avoid
- Changing shared settings without team approval.
- Saving incomplete fields.
- Using personal contact details instead of official office details.
- Forgetting to verify the impact on other teams.
