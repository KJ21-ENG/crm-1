# Dashboard Views
Status: completed
## 1) What this module is
Dashboard Views is your main work screen. It gives you a quick picture of what is happening today, what needs attention, and what is coming next.

## 2) What staff can do here
- See open items at a glance
- Check today’s priorities
- Track progress for leads, tasks, and tickets
- Open records directly from summary cards
- Adjust the view to focus on what matters most

## 3) How to use (step-by-step)
1. Open the Dashboard from the main menu.
![Screenshot](/docs/screenshots/13-dashboard-views/01-dashboard-views-overview.png)
2. Review the top summary cards first to see urgent counts.
3. Scroll through the sections for tasks, leads, and tickets.
4. Click any number or item to open the full list behind it.
5. Apply filters (for example: today, this week, my items).
6. Save your preferred view if that option is available.
7. Return to the dashboard several times during the day.

## 4) Important buttons/options
- Refresh: Updates numbers and lists.
- Filter: Shows only the items you want to see.
- Date Range: Limits results to a chosen time period.
- My Items: Focuses only on work assigned to you.
- View Details: Opens the full record list.

## 5) Daily best practices
- Start your day by checking the dashboard before other work.
- Re-check after lunch and before end of day.
- Keep filters simple so you do not hide important items.
- Use the same saved view each day for consistency.
- Follow up immediately on anything marked overdue.

## 6) Common mistakes to avoid
- Looking only at total numbers and missing urgent items.
- Forgetting to refresh before making decisions.
- Leaving old filters on and thinking data is missing.
- Ignoring overdue counts until they pile up.
- Opening too many records at once instead of working in order.
