# Login
Status: completed
## 1) What this module is
Login is the entry point to your work system. It confirms your identity and gives you secure access to your office tools.

## 2) What staff can do here
- Sign in to start work
- Reset password if access is lost
- Sign out safely when finished
- Keep account access secure

## 3) How to use (step-by-step)
1. Open the login page.
2. Enter your work email or username.

3. Enter your password carefully.
4. Select Sign In.
5. If asked for an extra verification step, complete it.
6. If login fails, use Forgot Password and follow the instructions.
7. After your shift, sign out before leaving your computer.

## 4) Important buttons/options
- Sign In: Accesses your account.
- Forgot Password: Starts password reset.
- Show/Hide Password: Helps you check typing accuracy.
- Remember Me (if available): Keeps you signed in on trusted devices.
- Sign Out: Ends your session securely.

## 5) Daily best practices
- Use a strong password and keep it private.
- Type slowly to avoid lockouts from repeated mistakes.
- Sign out on shared or public computers.
- Update your password when required by company policy.
- Report suspicious login activity immediately.

## 6) Common mistakes to avoid
- Sharing passwords with coworkers.
- Saving passwords on public or shared devices.
- Ignoring repeated failed login warnings.
- Leaving your account signed in when away from desk.
- Waiting too long to report access issues.
