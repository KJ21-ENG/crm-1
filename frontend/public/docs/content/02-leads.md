# Leads Guide for Office Staff
Status: completed
## 1) What this module is
The Leads module is where you track potential customers from first contact to qualification and follow-up.

## 2) What staff can do here
- Create leads, usually from `Call Logs` by opening a call detail popup and clicking `Create Lead`.
- Open a lead form with call details already filled (like phone number, caller name if available, and call context).
- Track lead progress with status updates.
- Assign leads by your office process (auto assignment, direct assignment, or assignment request).
- Log calls, emails, comments, notes, tasks, and attachments.
- Set and complete follow-ups.
- Record document/verification stages if your process uses them.
- Mark qualified leads and pass them for customer conversion.
- Delete leads only if your role allows it.

## 4) Important buttons/options
- `Create Lead` (in `Call Logs` call popup): start a new lead from that call.
- Prefilled fields in lead form: save time and reduce typing mistakes.
- `New Lead`: manual lead creation when needed (if not starting from Call Logs).
- `Filters` / saved views: focus on due follow-ups, stale leads, or qualified leads.
- `Status`: shows current pipeline stage.
- `Assign` / `Reassign`: set the person responsible.
- `Request Assignment`: request a specific owner when approval is needed.
- `Call`, `Email`, `WhatsApp`: contact lead from one place.
- `Add Task`, `Add Note`, `Add Comment`, `Attach File`: keep all work evidence linked.
- `Delete`: available only for authorized staff.

## 5) Daily best practices
- Start most lead creation from `Call Logs` so call details are prefilled automatically.
- Review all follow-ups due today before starting new outreach.
- Update status immediately after each interaction.
- Keep notes short, factual, and action-focused.
- Use tasks with due dates so no lead goes cold.
- Escalate qualified leads quickly to avoid delay.

## 6) Common mistakes to avoid
- Skipping the `Call Logs` popup flow and missing call details in the lead.
- Calling the same lead twice because of duplicate records.
- Leaving leads in `New` after contact already happened.
- Forgetting follow-up dates.
- Marking leads `Qualified` without enough details.
- Storing key updates outside the lead timeline.
- Deleting leads instead of using the correct status (when status tracking is required).
