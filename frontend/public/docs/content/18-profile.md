# 18. Profile
Status: completed
## 1) What this module is
Profile is where you manage your personal work details and account security, ensuring your team has the right contact info and your account remains secure.

## 2) What staff can do here
- Update their First and Last Name.
- Upload, change, or remove their profile picture.
- Check their registered email address.
- Change their account password for security.
- Keep personal details accurate for team communication.

## 3) How to use (step-by-step)
1. Open the user dropdown menu in the top-left corner (under the 'CRM' logo).
2. Click on **Settings**. This will open the Settings panel.
3. Select **Profile** from the left sidebar of the Settings panel.
![Screenshot](/docs/screenshots/18-profile/01-profile-settings.png)
4. Review your name and email. Update your First or Last name if necessary.
5. To change your picture, hover over your avatar and click **Upload image** or **Change image**. 

**To Change Password:**
1. While in the Profile Settings panel, click the **Change Password** button in the top right.
2. A prompt will appear asking for your Old Password and your New Password.
3. Fill in the required fields and confirm to securely change your login credentials.

## 4) Important buttons/options
- **Upload/Change image**: Allows setting a profile picture.
- **Change Password**: Opens the secure prompt to update your login password.
- **First name / Last name**: Text fields to edit your display name.
- **Update**: Saves any changes made to your name or profile image.

## 5) Daily best practices
- Use a clear, professional profile picture to help teammates recognize you.
- Always ensure your display name is spelled correctly.
- If you suspect your account is compromised, use the Change Password button immediately.
- Use a strong, unique password that you do not use on other platforms.

## 6) Common mistakes to avoid
- Forgetting to click the **Update** button after typing a new name.
- Trying to change the email address here (email addresses are tied to the base account and typically cannot be edited through this simple interface).
- Setting a weak, easily guessable password like '123456'.
