# Customers Guide for Office Staff
Status: completed
## 1) What this module is
The Customers module is the master record for active customers and their key details.

## 2) What staff can do here
- Maintain customer profiles. (Note: Customers cannot be created directly; they are auto-generated upon Ticket or Deed creation).
- Update name, contact, and identity details.
- View related lead/ticket/call history in one place.
- Open linked records quickly for context.
- Keep customer data accurate across ongoing support and sales work.

## 3) How to use (step-by-step)
1. Open `Customers` to search and view existing records.
![Screenshot](/docs/screenshots/03-customers/01-open-customers-search.png) <!-- apps/crm/docs/documentation creation task screenshots/03-customers/01-open-customers-search.png -->
2. Review the customer's complete contact, identity, and address details. (New customers are automatically created from Tickets or Deeds).
3. Review linked history (leads, tickets, interactions) for context.
![Screenshot](/docs/screenshots/03-customers/05-saved-customer-history.png) <!-- apps/crm/docs/documentation creation task screenshots/03-customers/05-saved-customer-history.png -->
4. Use edit options whenever customer details change (for example, updating PAN/Aadhaar or address).
![Screenshot](/docs/screenshots/03-customers/06-edit-customer-options.png) <!-- apps/crm/docs/documentation creation task screenshots/03-customers/06-edit-customer-options.png -->
5. Before calls or follow-ups, check the profile for latest context.
![Screenshot](/docs/screenshots/03-customers/07-customer-profile-context.png) <!-- apps/crm/docs/documentation creation task screenshots/03-customers/07-customer-profile-context.png -->

## 4) Important buttons/options
- `Filters` / list search: find records quickly.
- `Edit` / field update options: correct outdated details.
- Linked `Leads` / `Tickets`: open history for full background.
- Save/Update actions: keep profile current after verification.

## 5) Daily best practices
- Verify phone and email during every customer conversation.
- Keep legal/identity fields accurate and clean.
- Update profile immediately after receiving new documents.
- Use linked history before making commitments to customers.
- Keep one clean customer record instead of multiple partial records.

## 6) Common mistakes to avoid
- Entering identity numbers in the wrong format.
- Leaving outdated phone/email details.
- Ignoring linked history before responding.
- Making edits without confirming with the customer.
