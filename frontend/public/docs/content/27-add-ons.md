# Add-Ons
Status: completed
## 1) What this add-on is
The **call-log-mobile-app** add-on connects employee phone calling activity to CRM.
It is the mobile app required for teams that want call activity to appear in **CRM Call Logs**.

## 2) Why this add-on is required
Without this mobile app, CRM cannot receive employee call records from their phones.
This means call follow-ups, call history, and customer communication tracking stay incomplete.
Installing this add-on ensures calls are captured under the right employee account and visible in CRM Call Logs.

## 3) How employees install it (step-by-step)
1. Open **Add-Ons** in CRM.
![Screenshot](/docs/screenshots/27-add-ons/01-open-add-ons.png)
2. Find **call-log-mobile-app** in the list.
![Screenshot](/docs/screenshots/27-add-ons/02-find-call-log-app.png)
3. Select **Enable/Activate** for your organization.
4. Ask employees to install the mobile app from the approved company link or app source.
5. Each employee signs in using their own work CRM account in the mobile app.
6. Allow the app permissions it asks for (needed for call log sync).
7. Make one test call and confirm it appears in **CRM Call Logs**.
![Screenshot](/docs/screenshots/27-add-ons/04-confirm-call-logs.png)

## 4) How call sync works in daily use
- Each employee uses their own signed-in account in the mobile app.
- Calls made or received on that phone are synced to CRM.
- CRM records those calls in **Call Logs** under the same employee account.
- Teams can then see account-specific call history for follow-up and reporting.
- If someone is signed into the wrong account, call logs may appear under the wrong user.

## 5) Daily best practices
- Ensure every calling employee has the mobile app installed and signed in.
- Check regularly that new calls are appearing in CRM Call Logs.
- Keep employees signed in with their own work account only.
- Re-check app permissions after phone changes or app updates.
- Have team leads review call log accuracy during routine CRM checks.

## 6) Common mistakes to avoid
- Enabling the add-on but not installing the mobile app for employees.
- Sharing one account across multiple employees.
- Ignoring permission prompts that are required for call sync.
- Assuming calls are synced without doing a test call check.
- Not correcting wrong account sign-in when logs appear under another user.
