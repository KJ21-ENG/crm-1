# Email Templates
Status: completed
## 1) What this module is
Email Templates stores pre-written email messages for common office communication. Templates keep language consistent, professional, and policy-friendly.

## 2) What staff can do here
- Create standard messages for routine communication.
- Reuse approved wording for faster responses.
- Keep tone and formatting aligned across teams.

## 4) Important buttons/options
- **New**: Creates a new template.
- **Save**: Stores template changes.
- **Preview**: Shows how the email will look.
- **Template name**: Helps staff find the right message quickly.

## 5) Daily best practices
- Keep templates short, clear, and action-focused.
- Review templates regularly for policy and process updates.
- Use approved office language and branding.

## 6) Common mistakes to avoid
- Sending outdated template content.
- Using unclear subject lines.
- Adding sensitive information in standard templates.
- Editing approved templates without review.
